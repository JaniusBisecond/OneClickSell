## 中文介绍

<img width="469" height="67" alt="zh1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/edc30f76-87f6-4411-9d60-410f58b97c02" />



逃离鸭科夫Mod —— 一键出售。  
本 Mod 在商店界面里新增 “一键出售” 按钮，只需一键即可快速出售背包内所有物品。  
**功能特点：**
- 一键自动出售背包内容。
- 爱心标记的物品、锁定的格子以及现金不会被出售。
- **依赖组件：** 必须安装Harmony以确保本 Mod 正常运行。

## English Introduction

<img width="465" height="65" alt="en1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/11c09228-baed-4abf-a07d-5785a2e7875b" />


OneClickSell is a convenience mod for Escape from Duckov, enhancing your shop experience.  
With this mod, a “Sell All” button appears in the store interface, allowing you to instantly sell all items in your backpack with one click.

**Key features:**
- Instantly sell everything in your backpack, saving time.
- Items marked with a heart, locked cells, and cash will **not** be sold.
- **Required dependency:** Harmony must be installed for this mod to work.
